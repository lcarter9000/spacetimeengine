# Covariant derivatives (LaTeX):
\nabla_{r} v_{r} = 4
\nabla_{theta} v_{r} = - \frac{20 \theta}{r}
\nabla_{r} v_{theta} = - \frac{20 \theta}{r}
\nabla_{theta} v_{theta} = 4 r^{2} + 20

# Covariant derivatives (plain):
∇_r v_r = 4
∇_theta v_r = -20*theta/r
∇_r v_theta = -20*theta/r
∇_theta v_theta = 4*r**2 + 20
